About Consumer Behavior

Consumer Behavior examines how and why individuals select, purchase, use, and dispose of goods and services. Within this framework, Brand Loyalty influences repeat purchases by fostering a deep commitment to a brand; Buying Habits reveal the routine patterns that streamline decision‐making; Functional Design ensures that products meet users’ practical needs effectively; and Product Design shapes perceptions of quality and value. Incorporating Gamification elements can boost engagement and motivation, while Storytelling weaves emotional narratives that strengthen customer relationships. Underlying these dynamics, Psychological Factors—including perception, motivation, and learning—drive each stage of the buying process, and a well‐crafted User Experience Design integrates usability and aesthetics to deliver a seamless end‐to‐end interaction.


Reference: Solomon, M. R. (2018). Consumer Behavior: Buying, Having, and Being (12th ed.). Boston, MA: Pearson. ISBN 978-0134734825.

About this Project

This website was created as part of a student project exploring key aspects of Consumer Behaviour. Developed by a team of eight students, the site presents focused insights on topics such as Gamification, Functional Design, Buying Habits, User Experience Design, Psychological Factors, Product Design, Storytelling, and Brand Loyalty.
